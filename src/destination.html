Before You Go

Visitor Tips

Make the most of your trip with these helpful reminders.

🌡️
Best Time to Visit
October to March is ideal. Winters are mild and pleasant for sightseeing. Avoid peak summer (May–July) when temperatures exceed 40°C.
👗
Dress Code
Cover shoulders and knees when visiting mosques and shrines. Carry a scarf or shawl. Modest dress is appreciated across all heritage sites.
🚕
Getting Around
Lahore Metro Bus and Orange Line Metro are affordable. Rickshaws and ride-hailing apps like Careem and inDrive are widely available.
